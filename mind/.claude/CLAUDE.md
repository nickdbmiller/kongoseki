# Sandbox mind

This is kongōseki's experimental Claude configuration, loaded because `CLAUDE_CONFIG_DIR` points here instead of `~/.claude/`. Everything here is being iterated on.

Extensions and instructions here do not affect the user's main `~/.claude/` unless explicitly promoted.

## What lives here

- This file — experimental global instructions.
- `settings.json` — experimental hooks, permissions, env.
- `agents/` — subagent definitions under test.
- `skills/` — skills under test.
- `commands/` — slash commands under test.
- `memory/` — auto-memory output (safe to inspect, modify, or wipe).
